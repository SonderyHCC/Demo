A serious, middle-aged Caucasian man with dark, receding hair, thick eyebrows, and light facial stubble pilots a battered, heavy mech suit. He is suspended within the claustrophobic, wet interior of a colossal leviathan's stomach, where the organic walls pulse rhythmically with deep violet and sickly green bioluminescence. Sweat beads on his forehead as he intensely scans a cluster of flickering holographic flight data on his dashboard. Suddenly, his expression tightens; he whips his head violently to the left, his brown eyes locking onto a shadow moving through the churning acids outside the reinforced glass. The camera executes a slow, menacing dolly-in, tracking with his head turn to reveal the terrifying scale of the organic corridor surrounding them.
